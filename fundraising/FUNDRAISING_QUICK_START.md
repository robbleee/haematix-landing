## Week 2: Outreach & Meetings

### Day 8: First Outreach Wave (4 hours)
- [ ] **Warm intro requests** (3 hours)
  - Reach out to 5 connectors for warm intros to your Top 5 VCs
  - Use email template from UK_VC_OUTREACH_PLAN.md
  - Personalize each one
  - Make it easy for them (provide forwardable email)

- [ ] **Apply to Seedcamp** (1 hour)
  - Online application: https://seedcamp.com/
  - Use your exec summary as foundation
  - Upload pitch deck

### Day 9: Follow-up & Expand (3 hours)
- [ ] **Research 5 more VCs** (Tier 1B from plan)
  - Add to tracker
  - Find partners and LinkedIn profiles
  - Identify warm intro paths

- [ ] **Send 2 cold emails** (1 hour)
  - Only to VCs with clear healthcare thesis
  - Use cold email template from UK_VC_OUTREACH_PLAN.md
  - Personalize heavily (mention their portfolio/blog)
  - Attach deck

### Day 10-11: Prepare for Meetings (2 hours)
- [ ] **Update pitch deck** based on any new traction
- [ ] **Prepare custom slides** for each VC you're meeting
  - Create "Why [VC Firm]?" slide showing:
    - Portfolio synergies
    - Specific value they bring
    - Why you want them as partners

- [ ] **Research partners** you'll meet in depth
  - Read their recent tweets/posts
  - Note their investment thesis
  - Prepare smart questions to ask them

### Day 12-14: First Meetings (as scheduled)
- [ ] **Pre-meeting prep** (30 min each)
  - Review partner's background
  - Review their portfolio companies
  - Print out or have ready: Custom slide for this VC

- [ ] **Meeting execution** (45-60 min each)
  - Arrive 5 min early (or join Zoom 2 min early)
  - Let them guide pace
  - Listen more than talk
  - Take notes

- [ ] **Post-meeting follow-up** (within 4 hours)
  - Send thank you email
  - Recap key points discussed
  - Provide any requested materials
  - Propose next steps
  - Update tracker

## âš ï¸ Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Waiting for "perfect" materials** 
   - Ship at 80% quality, iterate based on feedback
   
2. **Only talking to one VC at a time**
   - Always have 5-10 active conversations
   
3. **Not tracking your outreach**
   - Use the tracker from day 1
   
4. **Being too available**
   - Don't reschedule your whole life for one VC meeting
   - Create healthy scarcity
   
5. **Giving up after no response**
   - 50% of emails get no response
   - Follow up once after 7-10 days
   
6. **Stopping traction while fundraising**
   - The best fundraising position is not needing money
   - Keep signing pilots, publishing results

## ðŸ†˜ If You're Stuck After 2 Weeks

### No responses to outreach?
- **Check:** Is your email going to spam? Test with a friend
- **Try:** More warm intros, fewer cold emails
- **Review:** Is your subject line compelling?
- **Consider:** Your LinkedIn profile might need updating

### Can't find warm intro paths?
- **Expand:** Ask co-founders to map their networks
- **Try:** NHS colleagues who've worked with startups before
- **Consider:** Fractional fundraising advisor (5% fee)
- **Alternative:** Apply to accelerators (Seedcamp, Entrepreneur First)

### Getting meetings but no follow-up?
- **Evaluate:** Score your last 3 meetings (use scoring system)
- **Ask:** Request honest feedback from friendly VC
- **Review:** Record yourself pitching, watch back
- **Consider:** Pivot messaging based on objections
