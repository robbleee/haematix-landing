## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips from the Trenches

### Week 1-2: Preparation
- **Tip:** Don't wait for perfect materials. Ship your deck at 80% quality.
- **Common blocker:** Analysis paralysis on which VCs to contact
- **Solution:** Just pick the top 5 from UK_VC_OUTREACH_PLAN.md and start

### Week 3-6: Outreach
- **Tip:** Personalize every single email. Generic = ignored.
- **Common blocker:** Can't find warm intros
- **Solution:** Ask co-founders, NHS colleagues, portfolio company founders

### Week 7-10: Meetings
- **Tip:** Listen more than you talk. Take notes. Ask smart questions.
- **Common blocker:** Not getting second meetings
- **Solution:** Use scoring system (VC_TRACKER_TEMPLATE.md) to improve

### Week 11-16: Closing
- **Tip:** Don't celebrate the term sheet. Deal's not done until money's in bank.
- **Common blocker:** Legal process taking forever
- **Solution:** Get startup-specialist lawyer (SeedLegals, Taylor Wessing)
