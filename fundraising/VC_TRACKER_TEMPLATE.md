## Scoring System (Post-Meeting)

After each meeting, score the VC on these criteria (1-5 scale):

### Interest Level (15 points)
- **Engagement** (1-5): How actively did they engage?
- **Follow-up commitment** (1-5): Specific next steps?
- **Timeline clarity** (1-5): Clear timeline expectations?

### Strategic Fit (15 points)
- **Healthcare expertise** (1-5): Domain understanding?
- **Portfolio synergies** (1-5): Value beyond capital?
- **Follow-on capacity** (1-5): Series A participation?

### Founder Fit (15 points)
- **Values alignment** (1-5): Trust factor?
- **Communication style** (1-5): Can you work together?
- **Expectations** (1-5): Realistic expectations?
